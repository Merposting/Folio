About me

Hi! I'm Sanvi, a passionate graphic design student with a keen eye for storytelling, culture, and visual communication. My work blends traditional and contemporary aesthetics, whether I’m designing a magazine, branding a fashion label, crafting a radio show identity, or illustrating narratives about constellations and folklore.

Currently, I'm part of the Digital Outreach Team for Radio KU 90 FM, where I create engaging social media content and host the Words and Waves book review segment. I thrive on conceptual thinking, bold creativity, and pushing the boundaries of design to create meaningful experiences.
